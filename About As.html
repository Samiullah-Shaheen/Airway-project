About Anadolujet

  • AnadoluJet, the successful trademark of Turkish Airlines, was established in 2008 to meet the air transportation needs of Anatolia with much more advantageous options and currently has 81 aircraft in its fleet, a network of 41 destinations, and 74 routes on domestic flights; 48 destinations, and 90 routes on international flights, in total 89 destinations and 164 routes.
  • AnadoluJet, which started international flights as of June 11, 2020, has direct flights to many domestic and international destinations from Istanbul Sabiha Gokcen Airport, Ankara Esenboga Airport and also Antalya, Bodrum and Dalaman. AnadoluJet, which operates its flights with the experienced cabin and cockpit crew of Turkish Airlines, will continue to serve in a wide flight network as the successful trademark of the flag carrier airline.
  • AnadoluJet offers its valuable guests a comfortable and affordable travel experience with complimentary treats, baggage services and car rental facilities. Adding value to the developing technology with its innovative approach, our brand continues to increase its service quality with opportunities such as "Extra Seat Selection" and "Meal Selection".
